# Code of Conduct

## The short version

Be respectful. Criticism of code, ideas, and technical decisions is welcome and expected. Personal attacks, harassment, and dismissive behaviour are not.

## Expected behaviour

- Give and receive feedback constructively
- Stay on topic — this is a technical project
- Assume good faith in others' contributions
- Accept that maintainers have final say on direction and merges

## Unacceptable behaviour

- Personal insults or attacks directed at contributors
- Sustained disruptive behaviour in issues or PRs
- Publishing others' private information without consent

## Enforcement

Violations can be reported by opening a private contact via GitHub. Maintainers reserve the right to close issues, remove comments, or ban users who repeatedly disregard these guidelines.
